I'm Steven. A self-taught Philippine web developer who got tired of watching great local businesses lose customers to competitors with flashier websites. So I fixed that.

My Story

From Tinkering to
National Scale

It started simple: a neighbor with a small cafe asked if I could help her get more customers online. I built her a website in a week. Within a month, her weekend reservations were fully booked.

Word spread. Salon owners. Gym managers. Auto shop operators. Restaurant families. They all had the same problem — beautiful businesses hidden behind either zero web presence or generic, template-looking sites that made them look amateur.

Today, Steven Designs operates as a one-man national-scale studio. I work directly with every client — no middlemen, no junior developers handing off your project. When you hire Steven Designs, you get Steven.
